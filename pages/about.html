About Me

A short biography of me

BIO

I was born in Jingdezhen, China. At 14-years-old I moved to Singapore and lived there for nearly a decade. I then shifted to Bonn and lived here for around three years. In fall 2019 I'll start my PhD in Göttingen with Thomas Schick.

My foremost interest is mathematics, more particularly heat kernel and all its surrounding topics. You can take a look of my interests for details.

I am also an enthusiast in deep learning, and am interested in WHY it works. Recently I have found some sound explanation.

Your can also download my PDF version of Vitae.
